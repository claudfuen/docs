<AccordionGroup>

<Accordion title="How can I contact support?">
  For support, you can reach us in several ways:

1. Email us at help@tryleap.ai (please include the email address associated with your account)
2. Check our documentation at https://docs.tryleap.ai

Our support team typically responds within 24 hours on business days. For urgent matters, please mark your email as high priority.

To speed up resolution, please include as much detail as possible when contacting us, and email us from the same email address associated with your account.

</Accordion>

<Accordion title="What if Leap doesn't have the integration that I want?">
  We offer customers the ability to request any integration they want, and we
  can typically ship these within 24 hours. Simply reach out to our support
  email at help.tryleap.ai and we'll prioritize getting that integration built
  for you.
</Accordion>

<Accordion title="Can I integrate Leap with my own systems?">
  Yes! We provide integration options in two ways: 1. A custom HTTP step that
  lets you connect to any API endpoint within your workflows 2. Our Leap API
  that allows you to programmatically trigger workflows from any external
  service or product and retrieve the results
</Accordion>

<Accordion title="How can Leap help me design and build workflows?">
  While we don't provide direct implementation services, our solutions team offers comprehensive guidance. For Business tier customers, we provide:
  1. 1-on-1 consultation calls to help spec out workflows
  2. Private Slack channels for ongoing support

We'll help you think through the design and recommend the right steps and actions to accomplish your goals.

</Accordion>

<Accordion title="Can I try Leap by myself?">
  Absolutely! We have a fully self-serve tier that you can sign up for and begin
  using immediately. We provide many templates to help you get started quickly
  and efficiently with your automation needs.
</Accordion>

<Accordion title="How many integrations does Leap support?">
  We support hundreds of integrations and are continuously adding more on a
  near-daily basis. Our extensive integration library ensures you can connect
  with virtually any service you need.
</Accordion>

<Accordion title="How secure is Leap AI for managing my data and workflows?">
  Your data's security is our top priority. We use:
  - Enterprise-grade encryption
  - Regular security audits
  - Compliant infrastructure

All data is encrypted both in transit and at rest, and we maintain strict access controls and monitoring.

</Accordion>

<Accordion title="How do I cancel my account?">
  To cancel your account:
  1. Click on your user icon (showing your name/email) in the bottom left corner of the dashboard
  2. Select "Billing" from the menu
  3. Look for the cancellation option in the billing page

If you experience any issues with cancellation, please contact us at help@tryleap.ai and we'll be happy to assist.

</Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>
