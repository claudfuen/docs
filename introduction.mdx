## FAQs

<AccordionGroup>
<Accordion title="How do I cancel my account?" icon="circle-xmark">
  To cancel your account:
  1. Click on your user icon (showing your name/email) in the bottom left corner of the dashboard
  2. Select "Billing" from the menu
  3. Look for the cancellation option in the billing page

If you experience any issues with cancellation, please contact us at help@tryleap.ai and we'll be happy to assist.
